Academic

From 2012 to 2018, I studied science in TsingHua high school in Beijing . At that time I was quite interested in history and physics. History is a subject to find the relationship between past and future while physicists explore the principle of the world. Moreover, I really enjoy the academic atmosphere in TsingHua high school where students were so willing to share their knowledge although they were in highly competitive positions.

Tsinghua High school teaching building

After I graduated from TsingHua high school in 2018, I went to Hong Kong Polytechnic University to obtain my bachelor degree. I major in electrical information engineering and minor in computer science. I choose my discipline because I have been a big fan of batman since I was very young and I want to use modern technology to build more powerful equipments.

Hong Kong Polytechnic University

In the past three years, I have studied many different aspects of electrical engineering, including communication theory, circuit design, embedded system, signal processing and control system. Since electrical engineering is a broad field, it is amazing to see how different areas integrated together. For example, we can design our control system and implement it in the micro controller to make the robot car go straight.

My control System in EIE3123

Besides my study in my EIE major,I am also interested in computer science and I have taken some computing subjects for my minor. Until now, I have learnt C, C++, Java, python, R and Matlab.Moreover, I have taken data structure and computer system subjects and now I am working on computer network. My study in computer science is beneficial to my study in my EE major as well because modern autonomous robotics require a lot of programming and computer vision knowledge.
